Content Warnings
We cannot stress enough: SLASHJAY CONTAINS DISTURBING AND SENSITIVE MATERIAL. While some potentially triggering scenes can be skipped, Slashjay has triggering content that is unavoidable.

Some unavoidable triggers are:
- Depictions of trauma and mental disorders,
- panic attacks/flashbacks (often related to PTSD),
- death (including child and animal death),
- murder,
- suicidal thoughts and ideation/tendencies,
- mentioned suicide attempts,
- childhood abuse and trauma,
- occasional mild gore (and rarely more graphic gore),
- mentions of alcohol and drug use,
- and slight adult material (swearing, innuendos, brief sexual comments or implications,).

Slashjay is not intended for children and is intended for an adult and teen audience. Themes of trauma, depression, survivor's guilt, serious mental health issues, and violence are all heavy in the comic and are, at times, centeral plot points. If you are in a dark place or are triggered by this content, DO NOT READ THE FUCKING COMIC.

In other words, Dead Dove: Do Not Eat
