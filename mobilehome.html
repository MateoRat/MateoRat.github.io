Slashjay, /j, or The Yassification of /j, is a brainrot self-indulgent FNaF comic/AU.

Slashjay takes place after Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach and follows Gregory as he falls down the horrific rabbit hole that is Fazbear Entertainment.

Slashjay is not a FNaF retelling, but a continuation/AU based off of Security Breach. It's a lot of brainrot, self-indulgent fanservice, silly goofy found family dynamics, angst, and character building. But, it also focuses on world and lore building, horror themes, mystery, and deep analyses on different topics. Slashjay also follows a rewritten timeline that Mike made for all his brainrot.
