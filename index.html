Slashjay, /j, or The Yassification of /j, is a brainrot self-indulgent FNaF comic/AU.

Slashjay takes place after Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach and follows Gregory as he falls down the horrific rabbit hole that is Fazbear Entertainment.

Slashjay is not a FNaF retelling, but a continuation/AU based off of Security Breach. It's a lot of brainrot, self-indulgent fanservice, silly goofy found family dynamics, angst, and character building. But, it also focuses on world and lore building, horror themes, mystery, and deep analyses on different topics.

ANNOUNCEMENTS
- 11/05/23 -
Howdy! An unfortunate...ish update.

Slashjay's development is going to be on hiatus for 2-6ish months. THIS ISN'T THE END OF THE COMIC OR OF DEVELOPMENT! It will be picked back up and when this hiatus ends development will pick up speed.

Now for the explanation and some elaborating. For those unaware, hi, I'm Mike, and I created Slashjay and have been the main person working on it. I want to address this as specifically coming from myself, rather than the entire team. I'm a senior in high school, and I'm currently swamped with work. I have nearly 2 years worth of school to finish this year in order to graduate, and I'm trying to knock through credits so I can apply to colleges without my application looking fucked up.

I'm in a place currently where I need to focus on school and finishing credits. Development will continue once I'm ahead/on track to graduate. So... do me a favor and hope for like 3 months.

Thank you to anyone who actually took the time to read this, you're so appreciated. If you want to try and support, join the Discord server!
