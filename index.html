Slashjay, /j, or The Yassification of /j, is a brainrot self-indulgent FNaF comic/AU.

Slashjay takes place after Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach and follows Gregory as he falls down the horrific rabbit hole that is Fazbear Entertainment.

Slashjay is not a FNaF retelling, but a continuation/AU based off of Security Breach. It will contain some pre-SB content, lore content, and other stuffs, though! It's a lot of brainrot, self-indulgent fanservice, silly goofy found family dynamics, angst, and character building. But, it also focuses on world and lore building, horror themes, mystery, and deep analyses on different topics. We're striving to build fanservice that's also a driven and growing plot. It's serial in nature and will have plot progression and changes along the way. Slashjay also follows a rewritten timeline that Mike made for all his brainrot. The general plot of FNaF is all still the same, dates have just been shifted around and changed to make more sense, as well as the addition of some character names, settings, etc.
